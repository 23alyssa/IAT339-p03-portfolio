I'm Alyssa!

I’m an enthusiastic designer, creator and human with a passion for design and a desire to make the world a little better each day.

A Little About my Journey

In my life I have had two big passions; art and technology. As a third-year student in SFU’s Interactive Arts and Technology Program, I have found a way to connect both these passions into design, by fusing creativity with critical thinking. I am able to apply software skills in Adobe Suite and Figma, as well as programming skills in HTML, CSS and Java to bring innovative projects to life. My goal is to use my design skillset to improve people’s everyday lives, focusing on the small moments in life that bring joy.

Aside from geeking out over layouts and design systems, I make the time for side projects that flex my creative muscles. Being a designer means more to me than just a career, which is why I’m passionate about designing outside the walls of a classroom. I volunteer as the sole Web and Graphic Designer at Emerge GX, a startup that hosts courses to learn professional skills. I also recently attended my first hackathon, where my project won Best User Experience/User Interface (UX/UI).

My Design Process

01

Ideation

Creating a design concept and deciding on scope sets the foundation of a project. This includes building a sitemap, creating user flows, and categorizing features.

02

Iteration

Finding the best design solution rarely happens on the first try. Its takes a lot of experimentation and iteration to find unique and creative solutions for complex problems.

03

Implementation

Considering the impacts of a project in use by prototyping, developing and testing ensures it can be applied to real world scenarios and promotes understanding.
